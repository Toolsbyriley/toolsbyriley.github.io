# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 13, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 13, 2026

---

## What We Do

We provide AI-powered business agents that help trade and construction businesses manage operations, generate estimates, track leads, and communicate with clients. Your agent runs on our infrastructure and communicates with you via Telegram.

## What We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Company information:** Business name, phone, email, license number, logo — used to brand your documents and proposals
- **Project data:** Blueprint PDFs, estimate inputs, bid amounts — used to generate and store your estimates
- **Conversations:** Messages you send to your agent — used to respond to your requests and improve service quality

### Data We Generate
- **Usage metrics:** Message counts, token usage, session timestamps — used for billing and service monitoring
- **Error logs:** Technical errors during agent operation — used for debugging and reliability
- **Estimate analytics:** Aggregate data like trade types and estimate counts (no dollar amounts) — used for product improvement

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Your client lists, contact databases, or CRM data (unless you explicitly ask us to manage leads)
- Your financial credentials, bank information, or accounting data
- Personal information about your employees
- Your pricing strategies beyond the estimates you generate
- Private messages not directed at your agent

## How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Deliver the service | Conversations, project data | Contract performance |
| Billing | Usage metrics | Contract performance |
| Improve the product | Anonymized usage patterns | Legitimate interest |
| Debug issues | Error logs | Legitimate interest |
| Generate your documents | Company info, estimate data | Contract performance |

**We will NEVER:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your project data to train AI models
- Share your data with other clients
- Use your business information for our own competitive purposes
- Access your data without a legitimate service reason

## Data Storage & Security

- **Location:** Your data is stored on our secured infrastructure in the United States
- **Isolation:** Each client's data is stored in a separate, sandboxed workspace. No client can access another client's data.
- **Transport:** All communications are encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
- **Access:** Only authorized personnel can access client data, and only for service delivery or debugging purposes
- **Backups:** Daily automated backups with the same security controls as primary data

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Conversation transcripts | 90 days (then auto-purged) |
| Estimates & project data | Duration of service + 30 days |
| Company settings & branding | Duration of service + 30 days |
| Usage metrics (anonymized) | 1 year |
| Error logs | 30 days |
| Uploaded files (blueprints, templates) | Duration of service + 30 days |

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access** — Request a complete export of all your data at any time
2. **Correction** — Ask us to correct any inaccurate data
3. **Deletion** — Request deletion of your data (we'll confirm within 48 hours)
4. **Portability** — Receive your data in a standard, machine-readable format
5. **Restrict processing** — Ask us to limit how we use your data
6. **Object** — Object to any data use based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, message your agent or contact us directly.

## Data Sharing

We use the following third-party services to deliver our product:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Anthropic (Claude) | AI processing | Your messages (processed, not stored by Anthropic per their API terms) |
| Google (Gemini) | Blueprint analysis | Blueprint images (processed, not stored per API terms) |
| Telegram | Message delivery | Messages in transit |
| Stripe | Payment processing | Billing information only |

We do not share your business data, estimates, or project information with any other parties.

## Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information, we will:
1. Notify you within 72 hours of discovery
2. Describe what data was affected
3. Explain what we're doing to resolve it
4. Provide guidance on any steps you should take

## Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of any material changes to this policy at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
